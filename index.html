Privacy Policy for Pomodoro - custom timer

1. Introduction

At Pomodoro - custom timer, we value the privacy of our users. This privacy policy explains the kind of information we store and how we use it.

2. Data We Collect

Pomodoro - custom timer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data externally for its core functionality. We only use AsyncStorage, a local storage solution, to remember your previous tasks. This data remains on your device and is not accessible to Pomodoro - custom timer or any third parties.

However, we use Google AdMob to serve advertisements in our app, which may collect and use certain user data to provide targeted ads.

3. How We Use the Data

The primary data stored by Pomodoro - custom timer, your previous tasks, is solely used to enhance your experience with the app by recalling your past tasks.

Google AdMob, which serves ads in Pomodoro - custom timer, uses certain data to provide targeted and relevant advertisements. You can find more about how Google uses your data here: Google's Privacy Policy

4. Data Sharing

We do not share or transmit any user-generated data with third parties. However, data used by Google AdMob for advertising purposes is processed according to Google's privacy practices.

5. User's Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your locally stored data at any time through the app's interface.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document.

7. Contact Us

For any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, you can contact us at techpietrek@gmail.com.
